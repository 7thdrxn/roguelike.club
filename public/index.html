It would be fun to have a community event celebrating Rogue, Nethack, Crawl, ADOM, Angband, and other roguelike games!

Let's make this happen on a Saturday in July 2016 in San Francisco: talks, playing together, strategy advice, helping each other try new games, speedruns to watch, and your favorite slime molds to eat.

For everyone, free or cheap to attend, whether you've played since 1987 or are curious but have never tried them (or got killed by an ant).

We're some friends working on this idea. Want to attend, give a talk, mentor fellow players, offer a free venue to use, or help in some other way?

Fill out the survey and put in your email!
