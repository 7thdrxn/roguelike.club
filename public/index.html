It would be fun to have a community event celebrating Rogue, NetHack, Crawl, ADOM, Angband, and other roguelike games!

Let's make this happen on a Saturday in July or August 2016 in San Francisco: talks, playing together, strategy advice, helping each other try new games, speedruns to watch, and your favorite slime molds to eat.

For everyone, free or cheap to attend, whether you've played since 1987 or are curious but have never tried them (or got killed by an ant).

We're some friends working on this idea. Want to attend, give a talk, mentor fellow players, offer a free venue to use, or participate in some other way? Fill out this survey and put in your email address!

Noah is the person plotting this

I'm a long time roguelike fan. I attended IRDC US this last year, and I'll be working on this along with some close friends in San Francisco. We want the event to be accessible, so we hope to make the event cheap or free - this is a labor of love. We've all have experience organizing things in the past and we're going to be working hard to make sure this event happens and is worth attending!
