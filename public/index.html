It would be fun to have a community event celebrating Rogue, Nethack, Crawl, ADOM, Angband, and other roguelike games!

Let's make this happen on a Saturday in late July 2016 in San Francisco, just for fun: talks, time to play together, strategy advice, helping each other try out new games, speedruns to watch, and your favorite food rations and slime molds to eat. For everyone, whether you'r e curious but have never played them or have played since 1987.
