Sponsorship

This Roguelike Celebration is a meeting of professional software developers, industry members in roles such as QA and tech writers, game enthusiasts, and more. We are meeting to celebrate our affection for roguelike games, with venue space generously donated by Eventbrite, and our event staff is 100% volunteer.

About our costs: There are several amazing people important to the history of roguelikes who are interested in speaking at this event, but they live in faraway places and they wouldn't be able to attend without assistance with travel costs. To provide compelling content, we need to bring these faraway speakers to San Francisco! Name tags, festive roguelike decorations, and after-party space add to our expenses.

We're expecting over 100 attendees interested in gaming, computer culture, and video game history. Can you or your company help make this celebration happen? If so, we want to help you reach our attendees.

If you're interested in any of this, please let us know by emailing contact@roguelike.club! Read about us if you’re curious who’s working on this.
