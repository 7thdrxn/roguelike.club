Sponsorship

This Roguelike Celebration is an entirely volunteer project that we're doing just for fun, with generously donated venue space. Would you or your company like to help make this event possible? Here are a few wonderful thing we'd like to be able to include at our event, which require more money than we can pay out of pocket.

Sponsor one, two, or three speakers - $400, $800, $1200

There are several amazing people important to the history of roguelikes who are interested in speaking at this event, but they live in faraway parts of the US or Europe and they wouldn't be able to attend without assistance with travel costs. We'd like to give a few hundred dollars to each faraway speaker to subsidize most of their plane ticket and hotel costs, according to what their costs are.

Sponsor decorations - $200

We'd love to be able to decorate the Eventbrite office to look like a proper dungeon-crawling event should look — candlelabras, tins of spinach, statues of centaurs... We could do a lot of amazing decorating with some clever shopping and about $200.
