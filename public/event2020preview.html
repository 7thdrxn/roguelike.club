Celebration 2020 Preview

Sign up for the preview! Get your ticket to the Celebration!

Roguelike Celebration 2020 Preview is scheduled Sunday August 30 2020 4:00 PM - 7:00 PM Pacific Time as a virtual event. It is free for all attendees, and all you need is a web browser!

Roguelike games have been part of gaming culture for over 30 years and they have a deep and special place in our hearts. We'd love to get to spend some time with you all learning about roguelikes and socializing together.

While the full Roguelike Celebration conference isn’t happening until October, we wanted to provide a space for the community to gather before then. This will be an opportunity to catch up with old friends, meet new ones, and explore our exciting custom-built multiplayer game/chat space! All you’ll need to attend is a web browser.

We're still finalizing the schedule, but we'll also have some of our speakers giving brief sneak peeks of what you'll get to see at the main Roguelike Celebration, October 3-4!
