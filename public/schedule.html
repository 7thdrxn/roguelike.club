Other activities

  • Depending on interest there may be a whole second track of talks!
  • Play workshops! There will be break-out rooms where you can dive deep into strategy and teach other experienced players
  • General play time! Perhaps try a game you haven't tried before, with the option of help from experienced players when you get stuck (or offer to help others). Plus: wiki editing time, collaboratively updating articles on Wikipedia and other popular wikis, such as NetHackWiki, Crawl Wiki, and RogueBasin.
  • Breaks with lots of time to chat! There will totally be amusing decorations to play with (like figurines of random animals), places to show off your favorite game, or make fake plastic amulets of yendor.
  • Comestibles. There will be snacks throughout the day: tins of spinach, pudding, eucalyptus leaves, fortune cookies, royal jelly :)
  • After the talks end we may change venues to go somewhere and eat/drink/hang out.
  • Have thoughts for other activities? Costume contest, a workshop on drawing dungeon scenes, story time? Get in touch and we'll try to make it happen - info{at}roguelike{dot}club
