Roguelike Celebration 2023

Online preview event!
September 10, 2023, 4:00PM - 6:00PM Pacific
Get a free ticket!

When and Where?

Roguelike Celebration 2023 will be held on Saturday and Sunday October 21st and 22nd, 2023 as a virtual event in our custom-built multiplayer game/chat space. Talks will also be streamed to Twitch and YouTube.

There will also be a free preview event September 10, 2023 in the same social space, with talks streamed to Twitch and YouTube

PDT CEST
(Mon)		 Preview Event Schedule
16:10 01:10 Introduction
16:15 01:15 David Brevik: a fireside chat style discussion about the development of Diablo
16:50 01:50 Nic Junius: "Play as in Stage Play: Designing Dynamic Narrative Moments Through Character Acting"
17:25 02:25 Aron Pietroń and Michał Ogłoziński (Eremite Games): a fireside chat style discussion about Against The Storm

If you'd like to submit a question for our fireside chats, you can do so at this form.

2023 Main Event Speakers & Talks

We're excited to announce our speaker lineup for our main 2023 event! A schedule will be posted nearer to the event.

Klingons, Hobbits, and the Oregon Trail: Procedural Generation in the First Decade of Text Games (more)

The author of "50 Years of Text Games" dives into some of the very earliest PCG in games from the 70s and the dawn of home computers, from one-liner simulations of cold weather geography to lossy compression of galactic sector maps far larger than 2K of memory could hold.
